About Me

Professional experience, enterprise delivery, and real‑world AI implementation.

I am an Azure Data Engineer with 5+ years of hands‑on industry experience building scalable ETL pipelines, enterprise data lakes, and analytics platforms using Azure Data Factory, Databricks, SQL, and Python.

Across my professional journey, I have worked on end‑to‑end data engineering solutions including ingestion, transformation, data quality validation, medallion architecture design, and production optimization for enterprise‑scale systems.

More recently, I have expanded into Generative AI and intelligent data applications, building Proof‑of‑Concept systems such as:

  • Enterprise RAG document chatbots
  • AI agents capable of reasoning and automation
  • GenAI‑driven data reconciliation and rule generation

My focus is to help companies move from raw data → intelligence → automation using a strong foundation of data engineering combined with modern AI capabilities.
